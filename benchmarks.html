💡 How to use this table

Take your base project cost from the benchmark table above, then add the relevant compliance lines. A $500K mid-market bank FinServ project with SR 11-7 + SOC 2 is really a $810K–$1.18M project. Most vendor proposals assume you already have these controls — you probably don't.

The regimes are real and named (SR 11-7, HIPAA, FDA SaMD, IEC 62443, GDPR/EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, SOC 2 — look them up). The dollar ranges are our estimates, informed by those frameworks and practitioner experience, not published figures from any single body. Pressure-test against your own compliance quotes.

Who built it

About

TrueAICost was built by Brian Crusoe — a manufacturing operations leader with 8+ years on production floors, a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt, and hands-on experience building data pipelines and analytics systems in food & CPG plants. It's the tool he wished existed after watching AI projects get scoped against build-only quotes and then quietly balloon.

How this is funded: it isn't. No AI vendor pays, refers, or sponsors anything here. There's no email gate and nothing for sale. It's an open-source side project — the entire model is public so you can verify it or fork it. Built for the operators who have to make AI work after the contract is signed, not the people selling it.

Cost ranges are documented heuristics, calibrated to published research (RAND, S&P Global, Gartner, McKinsey, Stanford HAI) and openly labeled where they're judgment calls rather than published figures.
